Smart Routing

Our sophisticated Smart Router offers connections to multiple Payment Gateways, Payment Processors and Acquirers. You can set multiple Acquirers for a Payment Method and Prioritize them. In case an Acquirer is down, payment request is routed to Acquirer who is next in priority. You can set BIN based routing rules. You may also route transactions based on BIN attributes. This allows you to cut-down Costs, as well as insulate yourself from acquirer downtimes.
